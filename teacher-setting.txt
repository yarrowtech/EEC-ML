# Teacher Settings Page (My Work Portal) - Feature Overview

This page, accessible at `/teacher/settings`, serves as a comprehensive "My Work Portal" for teachers to manage their professional administrative tasks.

## 1. Attendance Tracker
- **Daily Check-in/Check-out:** Real-time marking of attendance with timestamp recording.
- **Attendance Statistics:** Monthly summary showing:
    - Days Present
    - Days Late
    - Days Absent
    - Overall Attendance Rate (%)
- **Daily Details:** View daily entry time, exit time, and total working hours.
- **Attendance History:** A chronological log of daily records for the selected month, including check-in/out times and status.
- **Month Filtering:** Ability to view attendance records for different months.

## 2. Leave Management
- **Leave Requests:** Submit requests for various leave types:
    - Sick Leave
    - Casual Leave
    - Annual Leave
    - Maternity Leave
- **Leave Quota Tracking:** Real-time view of used vs. available casual leave days.
- **Request Lifecycle:** Track status of requests (Pending, Approved, Rejected).
- **Modification:** Edit or delete requests that are still in 'Pending' status.
- **Admin Feedback:** View notes/reasons provided by administration regarding leave decisions.
- **View Modes:** Toggle between List View and Grid View for managing requests.

## 3. Profile Management
- **Personal Information:** View and update:
    - Full Name
    - Email Address
    - Phone Number
    - Department
    - Join Date
    - Residential Address
    - Emergency Contact Information
- **Profile Picture:** Upload and update personal profile photo.
- **Professional Identity:** View read-only Employee ID/Code and Department.
- **Validation:** Visual indicators for saving states and success/error messages.

## 4. Expense Claims (Reimbursements)
- **Claim Submission:** Submit reimbursement requests with:
    - Category (e.g., Travel)
    - Amount
    - Date
    - Description
    - Receipt Attachment (Cloudinary upload support)
- **Financial Statistics:** View totals for Approved, Pending, and Rejected claims in local currency (INR).
- **History Tracking:** Log of all submitted claims with status tracking.
- **Modification:** Edit or delete pending claims.
- **View Modes:** Toggle between List View and Grid View for expense history.

## 5. Notifications & Alerts
- **Workflow Notifications:** Alerts for leave approvals, expense claim updates, and profile requirements.
- **Read/Unread Tracking:** Mark notifications as read to manage alerts.
- **Categorization:** Icons and colors for Success, Warning, and Info alerts.

## 6. Document Access
- **Policy Documents:** Access to Employee Handbook and other institutional policies.
- **Personal Records:** Download Salary Certificates, Tax Declaration forms, and Performance Reviews.
- **Document Management:** Support for both downloading institutional documents and uploading personal administrative files.
