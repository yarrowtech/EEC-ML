Teacher Dashboard Information List

Header Information:
- Greeting (Time-based: Good morning/afternoon/evening)
- Teacher Name & Initials
- Current Date & Time
- Class Teacher Allocation Label (e.g., "Class Teacher: 10-A, 10-B")

Quick Statistics (Summary Cards):
- Total Students (with percentage change and trend)
- Attendance Rate (with percentage change and trend)
- Pending Tasks (Evaluations)
- Upcoming Events

Quick Actions:
- Open Classes (/teacher/classes)
- Class Attendance (/teacher/classes/current/students/attendance)
- Assignments (/teacher/classes/current/assignments)
- Teaching (/teacher/classes/current/teaching)
- Assessments (/teacher/classes/current/assessments)
- AI Center (/teacher/ai-center)

Main Dashboard Sections:
1. Today's Schedule (Weekly Schedule):
   - List of upcoming classes with Subject, Class, Section, Room, Time, and Status.
   - Link to Full Timetable.

2. Performance Overview (Class Insights Snapshot):
   - Subject Averages with progress bars and trends.
   - Top Performers list with score and improvement metrics.
   - Timeframe filters: Weekly, Monthly, Yearly.

3. Recent Activity:
   - Timeline of recent updates/messages with relative timestamps.
   - Quick "Eye" icon to view details.

4. Upcoming Deadlines:
   - List of pending tasks/assignments with Type, Due Date, Title, Class, and Subject.

Utility Controls:
- Download Report
- Filter Data
- More Options (contextual menus)
- Timeframe Toggles (Weekly/Monthly/Yearly)
